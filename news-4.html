Croatian companies at the stone fair in Verona

For half a century, the Verona Stone Fair has been the venue for presenting the world's best manufacturing traditions and innovations

The Croatian Chamber of Commerce, Split County Chamber, this year, for the nineteenth time, organized a joint presentation of Croatian companies engaged in the extraction

and processing of architectural stone at the world's leading Marmo + Mac stone fair, which is being held from 27 to 30 September in Verona , Italy.

For half a century, the Verona Stone Fair has been the venue for presenting the world's best manufacturing traditions and innovations, international projects and initiatives promoting the specialty

and richness of natural stone and its potential in architecture and design.
