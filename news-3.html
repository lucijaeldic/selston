Quick and easy tips for cleaning granite

Granite cleaning can often be a problem, especially if you don't know what to use

Granite surfaces, like all other stone surfaces, are prone to staining. But there are effective ways to clean granite, and we have singled out the best for you.

Cleaning stains from different surfaces can often be a problem, especially if you don't know what to use. The easiest way to remove the liquid is to use only a paper towel. The trick is not to rub the surface, but to let the paper towel absorb the liquid. Then gently wipe the granite with a clean damp towel.
