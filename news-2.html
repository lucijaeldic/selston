Marble, a material that, whether you like it or not, you have to admire

If your marble is too expensive, it is good to know that there are faithful imitations, such as quartz and granite, which, for less money, will make a similar impression

Although marble has been used in interior design for centuries, its attractive pattern is now more popular than ever! This time, in addition to luxurious interiors, it is also used in modern, minimalist rooms that add a touch of luxury and unique personality.

Marble, in addition to the luxurious impression it achieves, has many other advantages. One of them is certainly its longevity and easy maintenance. Nevertheless, the care of marble surfaces requires some concessions.
