About Us

SELSTON d.o.o.

Sales and service of cutting and processing of granite, marble and other types of stone.

Polishing enhances the color of the stone and gives the surface a mirror-like shine, and the structure of the stone is fully expressed. Grinding produces a smooth surface with possibly very small radially spaced scratches.

The surfaces are smooth and matt, and the color and structure of the stone is pronounced. The natural cleaved surface of the face of the stone is obtained by machining whereby the cut stone elements are cut into a well or well.

The appearance of a flat stone slab obtained by sawing depends on the type of stone or sawing technology corresponding to the type in question. A newer way of processing architectural and building stone that enables the imitation of antique stone

surfaces is called waiting (antico) processing and is very demanding when lining the gardens and generally lining the outside horizontal spaces.
